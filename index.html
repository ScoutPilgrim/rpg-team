Drakania

Eons ago, the vicious Dragon Drakania fought a terrible battle with the seven gods of Norath. Climbing the frozen ramparts of Valhardor she slay the messenger god Herma and stole a nameless treasure from its crypts. Wounded in the fight she slithered back to her underground lair where her numberless brood awaited her return.


In the thousands years since that battle, no one who has entered her dungeon has ever returned...
